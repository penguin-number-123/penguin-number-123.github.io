a![n,b] = (a!b)!b)!b)...!b) with n !b's for n> 1
a![1,m,b] = a![m,b]
a![m,1,b] = a![m,b]
a![m,b,1] = a![m,b]
a![m,1,1] = a!m
a![n,m,b] = a![n-1,\(f(m),f(b)\)] , \(f(n) = n![n-1,n,n]\)
